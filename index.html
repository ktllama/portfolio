ABOUT

When the pandemic put an end to my career as an international educator and photojournalist, I embarked on a mission to find alternate ways to continue working in a global context. What better way to do this than to become a magician behind the scenes of the World Wide Web? I started playing around with CSS and HTML and suddenly everything clicked, my mathematical bilingual mind start churning with all that could be done with those two beautiful languages. My tinkering with friends websites and playing around with web design led me to an internship as the front-end developer for the local Habitat for Humanity. I noticed as I worked on these projects, it didn’t feel like work, but instead an adventure, an exciting experience unlocking keys to my own mind. Of course, the more I learned the more I realized I had a ways to go. So here I am, finished with certifications in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Python, PHP, Bootstrap, and jQuery among other frameworks. I am just beginning my career in this field but finding I have a knack and profound appreciation for web development. I hope to meet some cool mentors and continue producing interactive, responsive and innovative websites and programs. But above all I will continue learning about the profound world of of full-stack development and programming
